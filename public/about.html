About Yearbook 2020



Mission Statement

Yearbook 2020 was created during the COVID-19 quarantine. The goal of this project was to keep classmates, teachers, and friends connected dispite the abrupt end of the school year. As recent high school graduates, we wanted to recreate the high school tradition of yearbook signing while adhering to social distancing guidelines. Yearbook 2020 was the product of this goal.
